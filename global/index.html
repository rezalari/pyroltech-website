Where has the technology been built?

Each region taught the platform something different: fuel-quality standards in Europe, industrial scale in East Asia, hot-climate reliability in the Middle East. The result is a technology base proven under very different feedstocks, regulations and operating cultures, not a single-site prototype.

What is running right now?

A 2 MW plant in Turkey, running non-stop. It is a working commercial reference, not a pilot rig, and it is the fastest way to judge the technology on evidence rather than slides. We deliberately keep operating detail out of public pages; if you are evaluating a project, ask us and we will take you through the plant directly.

Why is Canada the current focus?

Canada has the three things this technology needs in one place: large, stranded residue streams from forestry, agriculture and municipalities; carbon policy that puts a real value on avoided and removed CO2; and research institutions willing to validate performance independently. Pyroltech is building Canadian research collaborations and first commercial projects now, on the same restrained principle as everywhere else: prove one plant brilliantly, then repeat.
