Pyroltech / Brand & Design Reference

The reference for every Pyroltech surface.

One source of truth for the website, pitch decks, proposals and documents. Click any swatch to copy its hex. Built from a study of 23 cleantech and carbon-removal leaders: warm, established, innovative.

Color

Warm light is the default. Deep forest green is the anchor. Antique brass is the value accent, used sparingly.

Typography

Fraunces (serif) for headlines, Inter for body, IBM Plex Mono for labels, specs and live numbers.

Components

Primary action = forest green. Value moment (final CTA, key numbers) = brass. Radius 10-16px. Soft shadow, no neon glow.

Card / tile

White surface on warm canvas, hairline border, soft shadow on hover.

2.5t

Key number in brass mono, tabular figures.

Forest accent

Headlines and icons use deep forest; links use emerald #1E7A52.

Imagery

Lead with real photography of real plants and product. Avoid stocky or low-res shots.

Do & avoid

Do

  • Warm light canvas, deep forest green, one disciplined brass accent.
  • Serif headlines, generous whitespace, real photos.
  • Brass only for value moments. Forest green for primary actions.
  • Keep 4.5:1+ contrast in both light and the forest-dark theme.

Avoid

  • Near-black backgrounds or neon lime in the default UI.
  • More than one accent colour, or loud glows and gradients.
  • Clinical pure-white canvas (use warm #F6F4EE).
  • Emojis as icons; use SVG line icons.
