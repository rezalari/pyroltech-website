Chapter 01 / The problem

Every year the world buries billions of tonnes of value.

Forests, farms, cities and industry generate mountains of residue. Most of it is hauled away, burned or landfilled, and the carbon, energy and nutrients locked inside are lost. The cost of removing it keeps climbing, and so do the emissions.

Chapter 02 / The shift

What if waste was not a cost, but a resource?

Pyroltech's thermochemical platform converts almost any organic or carbon-rich waste into clean carbon, fuels, energy and carbon-removal credits. The same tonne you used to pay to remove becomes a product you can sell. That is the flip at the heart of everything we do.

Chapter 03 / The solution

Waste in. Value out. On-site or at scale.

Our patented units run on their own recovered gas after start-up, from a portable skid you place on-site to full field-size plants. Pyrolysis, HTC, HTL, gasification and upgrading do the rest.
