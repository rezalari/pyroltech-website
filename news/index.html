Why is this page so quiet?

Deliberately. Cleantech has no shortage of announcements that outrun the engineering. Our rule is the opposite: execute first, announce second. When a project reaches the point where a customer, a regulator or a journalist could walk up and verify it, it appears here. Until then it stays a conversation. If you want the fuller picture behind any milestone, including the live plant, we would rather tell you directly than publish a version stripped of context; the Turkey 2 MW case study shows how much we can share when we sit down together.

How do press enquiries work?

One channel: the contact page. Tell us your outlet, your deadline and your angle, and we respond directly. We can provide company background, technical explanation in plain language, comment on waste-to-value and carbon-removal topics, and interviews with the founding team. For analysis pieces rather than announcements, our insights section is where we publish our own thinking.

A note on accuracy. If you are writing about us, we will help you get the numbers right. Every figure we stand behind publicly is on this site; anything else you may have seen deserves a check with us before print.
