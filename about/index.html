About

We turn the world's waste into value.

Pyroltech is a waste-to-value technology company. Our thermochemical know-how has been built and proven across six regions over more than two decades, and today we are bringing that expertise to Canada and North America, one focused project at a time.

Our belief

Waste is not the end of something, it is the start of something valuable. Every tonne of residue holds carbon, energy and nutrients worth recovering. Our job is to make that recovery clean, practical and profitable, right where the waste is created.

How we work

Start focused

We prove the numbers on one real project, then grow. Disciplined execution over grand promises.

Engineer for reality

Portable and field-size units matched to the feedstock, running on their own recovered gas.

Make it pay

Product revenue plus carbon-removal credits, so decarbonization is a business, not a cost.

Where we come from

Our technology draws on thermochemical engineering developed across Iran, Turkey, the Netherlands, Korea, Japan and China, and is now deployed and researched in Canada. That heritage is why our platform is broad and proven rather than experimental. Explore it on the interactive globe.
