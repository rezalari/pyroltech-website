Frequently asked questions

Why publish comparisons instead of just product pages?

Because most buying decisions are a choice between two options, not a search for one. These pages put the incumbent and the alternative side by side so the trade-off is clear before a call.

Are the figures on these pages guaranteed?

They are indicative benchmarks, drawn from published indices and Pyroltech operating experience. Every project is confirmed with its own spec, volume and quote.

What if my question is not covered here?

Check the glossary for definitions, or contact us. A short call with your feedstock or spec in hand usually settles a comparison faster than any page.
