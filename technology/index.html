Technology

One platform, four proven conversion routes.

Different wastes need different chemistry. Pyroltech runs the full thermochemical toolkit, matched to your feedstock and the products you want, and every unit runs on its own recovered gas once it is up to temperature.

The routes
Dry feedstock

Pyrolysis

Oxygen-starved heating that turns dry biomass and waste into biocarbon, bio-oil and syngas. The core of our biocarbon and wood-vinegar products.

Wet feedstock

Hydrothermal (HTC & HTL)

Processing in hot pressurised water, no drying needed. HTC makes hydrochar from sludge and manure; HTL makes biocrude from wet organics.

Gas route

Gasification & upgrading

Converts solid waste to syngas, then to hydrogen, renewable natural gas, methanol, ammonia or Fischer-Tropsch fuels.

Value stack

Carbon & nutrient recovery

Durable carbon locked as biocarbon earns removal credits; phosphorus and nitrogen are recovered from wet streams.

Two formats

Portable / skid

Containerised units placed on or near the waste source, so the carbon is made where it is used. Fast to deploy, ideal for remote sites and pilots.

Field-size plants

Full continuous plants for larger industrial volumes, turnkey or via technology licensing and EPC.
