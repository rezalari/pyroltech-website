What is metallurgical biocarbon?

It is biocarbon engineered for the furnace, not the field. By selecting clean woody feedstock and controlling pyrolysis temperature and densification, Pyroltech produces a carbon with high fixed carbon, low ash and low sulfur that behaves like fossil coke as a reductant and carbon source. Because the carbon is biogenic, every tonne that replaces coke removes the matching fossil CO2 from your books.

The business case

Two revenue effects move together. First, you displace coke you would otherwise buy, a benchmark near 240 to 290 dollars a tonne. Second, durable carbon removal at roughly 2.5 tonnes CO2 per tonne of biocarbon is credit-eligible at the current index of 130 to 165 dollars a tonne CO2, which can add several hundred dollars a tonne of value before buyer Scope 3 demand. We model the exact numbers for your site in a quote.

Proof point. A leading British Columbia smelter ran a 500,558 dollar CleanBC-funded biocarbon trial replacing metallurgical coal and coke, and Canada's NRCan CanmetENERGY Bio-Coke program has demonstrated charcoal in coke blends. The pathway is proven; the work now is matching spec and volume to your furnace.

Frequently asked questions

How is this different from ordinary biochar?

Soil biochar is optimised for porosity and nutrients. Metallurgical biocarbon is densified and tuned for high fixed carbon, low ash and low sulfur so it performs as a reductant in metal making.

How much coke can it actually replace?

Typically 20 to 30 percent of met coke or PCI without redesigning the process. Higher rates are possible with process tuning and trials.

Will it meet our furnace specification?

Yes. We tune feedstock and pyrolysis to hit target fixed carbon, ash, sulfur and the exact sizing or briquetting your process needs.

Can we earn carbon credits on top of the coke savings?

In most cases yes. Durable biochar carbon removal is credit-eligible at roughly 2.5 tonnes CO2 per tonne, a second revenue stream alongside the displaced coke.

Where is it produced?

From regional clean wood residue. Pyroltech can also place a portable unit on or near your site so the carbon is made where it is used.
