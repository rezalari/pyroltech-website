Privacy policy

What we collect. When you use the contact form or email us, we receive what you send: typically your name, email address, company and message. Our web server also keeps standard technical logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for security and troubleshooting.

Why we collect it. To answer your enquiry, prepare quotes and feasibility work you ask for, and keep the site running securely. That is the whole list. We do not build advertising profiles and we do not sell or rent personal information to anyone.

Who we share it with. Only the service providers we need to operate, such as website hosting and email. They process data on our instructions and cannot use it for their own purposes. We may also disclose information if the law requires it.

How long we keep it. Correspondence is kept for as long as the conversation or project it relates to stays live, plus a reasonable period afterwards for records. Server logs are kept for a short rotation period and then deleted.

Your rights. You can ask what personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it where we have no continuing need to keep it. Write to info@pyroltech.com and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Children. This site is aimed at businesses and professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Cookies

What we set. Essential cookies only, the ones the site needs to function, plus, where enabled, a privacy-respecting analytics tool that counts visits in aggregate. We do not set advertising or cross-site tracking cookies, and we do not use social-media pixels.

Managing cookies. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time. Because we only use essential and aggregate-analytics cookies, blocking them will not break anything important on this site.
